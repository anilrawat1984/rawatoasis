Luxury Meets Serenity

Rawat's Oasis is a private, independent homestay designed for the discerning traveler seeking solace without sacrificing comfort. Our property in **Veerbhadra** offers a genuine, unplugged Rishikesh experience, removed from the crowds yet conveniently located near spiritual landmarks.

We offer two distinct, luxuriously appointed spaces: a generous **1BHK on the first floor** and a sophisticated **Studio Apartment on the second floor**. This dual configuration makes our oasis ideal for large families, two couples, or private, extended stays.

Every stay includes exclusive access to two vast **terraces** offering breathtaking, unobstructed mountain vistas. The soothing sound of a stream fed by the Ganges, just a 500-meter walk away, completes this unique setting. Experience exceptional service from your host, **Anil**, with seamless self check-in provided.

Accommodation: Sleeps up to 7 guests, 2 bedrooms (King Beds), 3 bathrooms.
